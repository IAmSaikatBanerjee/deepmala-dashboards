🪔 Deepmala — Song List

All mantra text is ancient public domain. The audio you generate in Suno is your own original recording. Kirtan style = lead voice + chorus answering, so it stays melodic and loop-worthy.

⚙️ Do this ONCE in Suno (before you start)

  1. Open suno.com and sign in.
  2. Click Create.
  3. Turn Custom mode ON. Now you see a Lyrics box and a Styles box.
  4. Turn Instrumental OFF. (Only for the "Flute" song turn it ON.)
  5. Pick the highest model available to you (e.g. v4 / v4.5).
  6. Open Advanced Options → set Style Influence 85% and Weirdness (Creativity) 15%. This makes BOTH clips come out consistently good.

▶ For EACH song

  1. Copy STYLE → paste in Suno Styles box.
  2. Copy LYRICS → paste in Suno Lyrics box. Paste it 10 times in a row (paste, paste, paste... 10x) — this makes the song 7+ minutes long instead of just 1 minute.
  3. Click Create. Keep the better of the 2 clips. Download it.
  4. In Filmora: repeat the clip until 1 hour (8 hours for song 1 Sleep). Add one image. Export.
  5. Upload to YouTube. Open the song's "YouTube" line here for the title, tags, description. Set AI content = YES.

Note: odd hyphen spellings like Jagan-naath are intentional — they make Suno pronounce Sanskrit correctly. Paste them as-is, don't "correct" them.

Thumbnail: copy the THUMBNAIL PROMPT block into Google AI (Gemini/ImageFX). Generate at least 2 images and pick the better one — just like Suno. Keep at least half the image as clean, uncluttered space — that's where you'll add bold text over it later if needed. A warm smiling face + soft glow = higher clicks. If an image gets refused or looks off, generate again — the wording is written to avoid that, but AI image tools can still be inconsistent with religious figures.
